    Logistic Regression from Scratch in C++WIP

    July 2026 · Series overview

    Work in progress. This sub-series builds logistic regression in C++ from the sigmoid and log-loss through Newton/IRLS solvers, regularization, multiclass softmax, and calibrated evaluation. The pages below are placeholders.

    Series Roadmap

    • Sigmoid, Log-Loss, and Gradients: the model, the loss, and its gradient.
    • Newton's Method and IRLS: second-order optimization for logistic regression.
    • Regularized Logistic Regression: L2 and L1 penalized classification.
    • Multiclass Softmax Regression: generalizing to more than two classes.
    • Classification Metrics and Calibration: accuracy, precision, recall, ROC, and calibration.
