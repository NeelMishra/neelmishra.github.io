    Linear Regression from Scratch in C++WIP

    July 2026 · Series overview

    Work in progress. This sub-series builds linear regression in C++ from the closed-form solution outward to numerically stable solvers, regularized variants, robust losses, and evaluation. The pages below are placeholders.

    Series Roadmap

    • Ordinary Least Squares: the normal equations and a pivoted solver.
    • QR and SVD Least-Squares Solvers: numerically stable alternatives to the normal equations.
    • Gradient Descent: batch, mini-batch, stochastic, and online updates.
    • Ridge, Lasso, and Elastic Net: penalized coefficient estimation.
    • Robust and Quantile Regression: Huber, LAD, and pinball loss.
    • Diagnostics and Metrics: residual checks, leverage, and evaluation.
