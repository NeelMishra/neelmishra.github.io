    Segment Trees

    March 2026 · 4 min read

    Segment trees are a fundamental data structure for handling range queries with updates in O(log n). They appear everywhere: competitive programming, database indexes, computational geometry, and interview problems. This series covers segment trees from the ground up, with detailed explanations, step-by-step walkthroughs, and interactive animations.
