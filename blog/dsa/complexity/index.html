    Time & Space Complexity

    March 2026 · 4 min read

    Every algorithm has a cost — how much time it takes and how much memory it uses. Understanding, deriving, and comparing these costs is the most fundamental skill in computer science. This series covers complexity analysis from the ground up: formal definitions, mathematical derivations, proof techniques, and practical pattern recognition.
