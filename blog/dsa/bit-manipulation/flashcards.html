    Bit Manipulation Flashcards

    June 2026 · Interactive revision

    How to Use This Page

    Select a bit manipulation topic, choose concept cards, practice cards, or a mixed deck, then generate a random card. Concept cards test formulas, invariants, and recognition rules. Practice cards tell you which problems to solve for quick revision and what pattern each problem is meant to reinforce.

    Best revision loop: try to answer the front of the card aloud, flip it, then solve one listed practice problem without looking at notes.

    What the Topics Cover

    Core operations

    AND, OR, XOR, NOT, shifts, precedence, and identity recall.

    Masks and subsets

    Represent sets as integers, enumerate masks, and recognize O(3^n) submask loops.

    XOR family

    Cancellation, prefix XOR, binary tries, and linear basis over GF(2).

    Advanced CP

    Bitmask DP, SOS DP, FWT, bitset optimization, digit DP, and Grundy XOR.
