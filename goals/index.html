Principles

Non-negotiable rules that protect the focus and energy the goals above depend on.

  • 1

    No screen time over 2 hours daily

    Keep non-work, recreational screen time under 2 hours a day. Study/coding hours for the goals above do not count; this cap is for leisure browsing, videos, and social media.

  • 2

    No gaming or motivation videos, no gaming music

    No watching gaming-related videos, no watching motivation videos, and no listening to gaming music. Remove the triggers that pull focus back toward gaming and replace real work with empty hype.

  • 3

    One set of reps every 45 minutes

    Every 45 minutes of work, get up and do one set of a body-weight exercise: push-ups, crunches, squats, lunges, or planks. Rotate the movement each break to keep the body moving through long study blocks.
